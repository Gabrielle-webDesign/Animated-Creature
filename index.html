The Undead Trick-or-Treater

Jacko Phantom

JACKO PHANTOM is a whimsical little creature.

A ghost boy donned in a bleached onesie and headed with a freshly carved pumpkin.

This little trickster still enjoys roaming the foggy streets and cold fields on crescent moon nights, a single roman candle to light his way, gathering candy to satisfy his still very alive sweet tooth. That purple sack with a yellow pie is bottomless.

How did Jacko Phantom get this way? No one knows, but everyone knows that if you give him a cherry lolipop, his all-time favorite candy, he'll make his head float for you!

"A literal trick for a treat!" Oh, that little Jacko Phantom.
